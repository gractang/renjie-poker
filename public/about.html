The Game

Renjie Poker was created by Renjie You, a trader at Jane Street Capital . I first came across this game from some of my friends who had interned at Jane Street, and it quickly became a favorite pastime of mine. Despite the game having a "player" and a "dealer," it's actually functionally a single-player game, which makes it a great candidate to stave off boredom.

One of the interesting things to me about Renjie Poker is that I don't think (to my knowledge) there's a solved optimal strategy for the game. Renjie was heavily involved in the internship program, and as such I had the distinct pleasure of playing with him in person while interning at Jane Street! When chatting with him about strategies, he estimated that with optimal play, the player can expect to win around 60% of the time. I encourage you to see if you can get close to this rate!
