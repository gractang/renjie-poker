About Pick'em Poker

The Game

I learned about this game at Jane Street. Despite the game having a "player" and a "dealer," it's actually functionally a single-player game, which makes it a great candidate to stave off boredom.

One of the interesting things to me about pick'em poker is that I don't think (to my knowledge) there's a solved optimal strategy for the game. With strong play, a high win rate seems achievable. I encourage you to see if you can get close to 60%!
